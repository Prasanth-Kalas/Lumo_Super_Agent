# Using Lumo

This section is for people using Lumo to get work done — not building agents, not running servers, just talking to Lumo and expecting it to deliver. Start at the top and work down, or jump to whatever you're trying to do.

## In this section

- **[What is Lumo?](what-is-lumo.md)** — one-minute overview of what Lumo does and why it exists.
- **[Getting started](getting-started.md)** — signing up, your first conversation, what to expect.
- **[Connecting your apps](connecting-apps.md)** — Marketplace, OAuth flow, Google / Microsoft / Spotify, disconnecting.
- **[Voice mode](voice-mode.md)** — hands-free mode, wake word, voice picker, what to do if the mic isn't working.
- **[Memory — what Lumo remembers](memory.md)** — the profile, facts, behavior patterns, and the "Forget everything" switch.
- **[Standing intents](standing-intents.md)** — recurring jobs Lumo runs on your behalf (the "wake me when something changes" loop).
- **[Notifications](notifications.md)** — the bell, proactive nudges, how often you'll hear from Lumo.
- **[Autonomy — how independent Lumo is](autonomy.md)** — tiers, spend caps, the kill-switch, review history.
- **[Privacy — what's stored, what's not](privacy.md)** — a plain-English contract about your data.
- **[Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md)** — common issues and how to fix them.
